Keep your teeth, and the planet, clean. Get an oral care product that says you care about the earth. A biodegradable toothbrush made from organically grown bamboo. With the added benefit of being better for the environment, Rusabl bamboo toothbrush cleans just as well and lasts as long as your plastic toothbrush.

Usage Instructions

The switch to an Eco friendly and sustainable oral care made easy by Rusabl. Keep the following points in mind to get the best of our biodegradable bamboo toothbrushes :

  • Apply toothpaste on your wooden toothbrush like you normally would.
  • Wipe the handle with your hand to remove excess water on the handle and let it dry. 
  • We recommend storing your Eco friendly brush in a dry stand/container to reduce contact with moisture and avoid holders that have a tendency to collect water.Use Rusabl toothbrush stand for best results.
  • When replacing your wooden toothbrush after 3 months, remove the bristles using pliers. The bare bamboo will safely breakdown into the soil within 6 month.

Dentist recommends replacing your toothbrush every 3 months or when the bristles appear frayed.

By the time you dispose off your wooden toothbrush, a bamboo tree matures and grows up to 90 feet, thereby reducing your carbon footprint and also acting against climate change. 

Your Impact

Bamboo toothbrushes are a sustainable alternative to plastic toothbrushes that continue polluting our oceans, marine life and the planet decades after being discarded. Unlike plastic toothbrush, your wooden toothbrush will disintegrate into the soil in a span of 6 months.

With this switch, you make sure that you don’t contribute to this pile of plastic waste in the landfill anymore.

Here are the answers to the most frequently asked questions

  • Why bamboo toothbrushes?

  • Plastic brushes find their way into the oceans, destroying marine life and the planet decades after it is discarded. Bamboo toothbrushes are a sustainable alternative to plastic brushes. They disintegrate in just six months.

    Of course, they clean just as well and last just as long as normal plastic toothbrushes.

  • What are the bristles made off?

  • As the only biodegradable option is boar hair, we decided to use nylon bristles on our toothbrushes. We love animals as much as we love the environment, so using boar hair was an absolute no-no. We have been experimenting with other types of bristles, but no other material lasts long. While we continue to find a sustainable alternative, your ideas would be highly appreciated.

  • How is chopping bamboo any different from chopping trees?

  • Rusabl brushes are made from Moso bamboo, which is one of the fastest growing plants. It will regrow by the time you dispose of your bamboo toothbrush.
